Preliminary Questions

We need to ask some questions to check that you qualify for this product. Please answer all the questions honestly and to the best of your knowledge. If you don't, your policy may be cancelled or claim rejected or not paid in full.

  1. Model Insurance only offer comprehensive cover - is that ok
  2. Are you the main user of the car
  3. Do you have the car owner's permission to fit a telematics box and do you and any named drivers agree to Model Insurance using and processing any recorded driving data
  4. Is the vehicle owned by you, your parent, grandparent, husband/wife or civil partner
  5. Have you or any other drivers of the car you want to insure:
    1. Had any restrictions added to your/their driving licence by the DVLA due to any disability or medical condition
    2. Been refused, had cancelled or had any special terms imposed on previous insurance policies
    3. Had any non-motoring convictions that are not spent under the Rehabilitation of Offenders Act 1974 or any convictions for help
  6. Has the car been changed from the manufacturer's standard specification?
  7. Will the vehicle be used for any commercial purposes including private hire, driving tuition, rallies and anything to do with the motor trade
