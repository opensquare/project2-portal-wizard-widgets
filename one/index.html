The Service Plan product is designed to ensure that you, the motorist, have peace of mind when it comes to the expenses associated with servicing your vehicle. The Service Plan is designed to minimize the financial impact when the vehicle is required to be serviced as specified by the manufacturer.

Benefits are as follows:

  • You will not be presented with an expensive bill to pay on the spot when you collect your vehicle at the dealership
  • The replacement of oil, oil filters, sump plug gaskets and the cambelt is covered as part of the manufacturer's stipulated miles
  • Included in the cost of the plan are parts, labour and VAT
  • Costs are spread over a term or period to allow an affordable premium
  • Future servcing costs are protected against the impact of inflation

The Service Plan offers convenience from when you book your vehicle in at an authorised service centre to when it is collected. Once you inform the dealer that you have a Service Plan, the dealer/garage will get system authorisation on your behalf to continue with the service.
